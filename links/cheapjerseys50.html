(AP) Rex Ryan bolted out of his office the moment he heard Ed Reed was cut.He wanted to make sure general manager John Idzik knew one of his favorite players was available for the New York Jets to pursue."It's only a rumor that I pulled a hamstring," Ryan said, grinning. The 35-year-old Reed was released Tuesday by Houston, cleared waivers Wednesday and joined less than 24 hours later."John had a big smile on his face," Ryan said about the Jets' GM. "I think he anticipated it, but he had already beaten me to the punch. You'll see."You guys can be the judge of it."The Jets released outside linebacker Ricky Sapp to make room on the roster for Reed.Ryan and defensive coordinator Dennis Thurman coached Reed for eight years in Baltimore. Reed has always been a favorite of Ryan, who once labeled him the greatest safety to play the game.
