About Linda Jenkins
Linda Jenkins is a travel writer and photographer and contributes travel articles to Johns Creek Living Magazine, Buckhead Living Magazine and The Sunday Paper (Atlanta). Currently, she serves on the Board for Newtown Park in Johns Creek, Georgia. Her company, Vacations-Abroad.com, provides wine and garden tours to Italy and France.
