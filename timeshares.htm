International Destinations the Norm?

 You've traveled internationally enough times to know that you'll find another must see destination every year. Why not make things easier on yourself by owning an international timeshare?

Why a Timeshare is for You

Whether you want to escape to Dubai to mingle with princesses and sheiks or discuss literary trends at a Prague café with ex-pats, consider owning a vacation property. Every year you'll have piece of mind knowing your accommodations are set. You won't have to worry about what inflation will do to hotel prices -you've already purchased your suite. Additionally, if you purchase through an exchange company, such as Resort Condominiums International, you won't be tied to one locale. You'll have thousands of resorts to choose from. Another bonus is that RCI rates resorts and lists customer comments and reviews to help you find just the right accommodations for you.

If you're still unsure about the timeshare industry, start by surfing through online forums. There are a variety available. One thing's for sure; you'll find many frank discussions regarding everything you need to know.

If you find a resort you're interested in, consult its timeshare calendar to find what they determine to be their busiest season. Keep in mind that while you may save money by choosing a less desirable season, if you sell your timeshare, you'll reap a lower sales price as well.

If you don't wish to purchase through an exchange company, try to find a company with resorts in international destinations, like Diamond Resorts International. Many have in-network exchange programs, which will allow you a lot of choice as to where you stay.

Don't underestimate the value of having your own little piece of home to return to during an international trip. It can be enormously comforting to have not just a comfortable bedroom, but a living room and kitchen where you can prepare a simple breakfast or midnight snack. Most timeshares offer these amenities as well as others, like on-site water park complexes, tennis courts, golf courses, and discounts to area attractions.
