Koh Samui, the island of the coconut trees, is the third largest island in Thailand. Well known beaches are Chaweng, Lamai, Bophut, Big Buddha, Choeng Mon, Hua Thanon and Maenam.

There were no roads or cars on Samui until early 1940's. Samui is now the second most popular island destination in Thailand, after Phuket. Ko Samui is an oasis of natural beauty, with its white sandy beaches,  dazzling coral, luscious lagoons, picturesque waterfalls, swaying coconut trees and crystal clear water.
