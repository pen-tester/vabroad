Basilica di Santa Maria del Fiore

Battistero di San Giovanni, or the Baptistry of St John is in the same plaza as the Duomo. This building is done in a typical black and white marble that is prevalent in Italian churches. The Baptistry is considered one of the oldest buildings in Florence.
